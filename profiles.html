Profiles of Computational Biologists & Bioinformaticians

The terms "computational biology" and "bioinformatics" have been used interchangeably to describe research spanning a broad range of disciplines in the biological sciences. We profiled several scientists using computational and quantitative skills to demonstrate the diverse research questions addressed in this field.
