Where can I study computational biology?

Here is a short list of places in the US where you can formally study computational biology. This may give you an idea of the diversity of degree programs in bioinformatics and computational biology. More comprehensive lists are provided by the International Society for Computational Biology and bioinformatics.org:

  • https://www.iscb.org/iscb-degree-certificate-programs
  • http://www.bioinformatics.org/wiki/Education_in_the_United_States
Additionally, we encourage you to investigate degree programs in the biological sciences, computer science, and statistics. Many computational biologists earn their degrees in programs without a formal bioinformatics or computational biology emphasis. And check out these resources to find labs that are actively recruiting students:
  • The Evolution Directory
  • ESA listserv
